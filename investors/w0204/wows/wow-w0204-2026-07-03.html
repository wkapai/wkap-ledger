Stablecoin revenue architecture

Reserve-yield sharing may shift from selective distribution weapon to industry baseline.

Why now: The market is debating whether stablecoin issuer margins are durable or structurally compressing.

Evidence to watch: Track issuer disclosures, exchange renegotiations, wallet incentives, reserve-income take rates, and new stablecoin launch terms.

WOW-2026-07-03-002

Coinbase stablecoin economics

Coinbase-linked stablecoin economics may be more sensitive to partner take-rate renegotiation than headline supply growth.

Why now: Public-market discussion is shifting from circulation to revenue rights.

Evidence to watch: Track Coinbase filings, USDC agreement language, and management commentary on revenue share.

Source refs: Reading Item 1

WOW-2026-07-03-003

Payment infrastructure

Payment platforms may compete on who captures reserve income instead of who owns the customer interface.

Why now: Stablecoin economics are becoming an infrastructure pricing question.

Evidence to watch: Track merchant adoption, wallet payout incentives, and reserve-yield distribution terms.
